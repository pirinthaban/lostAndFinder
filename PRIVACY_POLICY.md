## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

**Encryption:**
- Data in transit: TLS 1.3 / HTTPS
- Data at rest: Firebase encryption
- End-to-end chat encryption: AES-256
- Password hashing: bcrypt / scrypt

**Access Controls:**
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Multi-factor authentication
- Regular security audits
- Firestore security rules

**Application Security:**
- Input validation and sanitization
- SQL injection prevention (NoSQL database)
- XSS protection
- Rate limiting and DDoS protection

**Image Security:**
- Automatic blurring of sensitive data (NIC numbers, faces)
- Content moderation and scanning
- Malware detection

### 4.2 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach:
- We will notify affected users within 72 hours
- We will report to relevant authorities as required
- We will take immediate steps to mitigate harm
- We will provide guidance on protective measures

### 4.3 Your Security Responsibilities

You are responsible for:
- Keeping your login credentials confidential
- Not sharing your account with others
- Using strong passwords
- Enabling two-factor authentication
- Reporting suspicious activity immediately

**We will never ask for your password via email or phone.**

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Retention Periods

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|------------------|--------|
| **Active account data** | Until account deletion | Service provision |
| **Active items** | Until deleted or 90 days inactive | Recovery process |
| **Closed/recovered items** | 30 days after closure | Dispute resolution |
| **Chat messages** | Until both parties delete | Communication history |
| **Deleted accounts** | 30 days | Recovery option |
| **Audit logs** | 2 years | Security and compliance |
| **Anonymized analytics** | Indefinitely | Service improvement |
| **Backup copies** | Up to 30 days | Disaster recovery |

### 5.2 Account Deletion

When you delete your account:
1. Your profile is immediately deactivated
2. Personal data is anonymized after 30 days
3. Items are marked as deleted
4. Messages remain visible to other participants (anonymized)
5. Audit logs are retained for legal compliance (2 years)
6. Anonymized analytics may remain indefinitely

### 5.3 Right to Data Deletion

You may request deletion of specific data by:
- Deleting items individually in the app
- Deleting your entire account in Settings
- Contacting support for special requests

**Note:** Some data must be retained for legal or security purposes (e.g., records of fraudulent activity).
