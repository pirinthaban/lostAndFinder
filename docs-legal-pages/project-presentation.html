1. Project Overview

FindBack is a comprehensive cross-platform application designed to simplify the process of reclaiming lost items. By connecting people who have lost belongings with those who have found them, FindBack creates a trustworthy community network driven by advanced technology.

2. Problem Statement

The Challenge: Losing valuable items (wallets, phones, documents) causes significant stress and chaos. Traditional lost-and-found systems are fragmented, physical-location dependent, and lack efficient search capabilities, making recovery difficult and unlikely.

Current solutions often rely on disconnected social media posts or physical lost-and-found bins, leading to a lack of centralized data and privacy concerns for users sharing personal contact details publicly.

3. Proposed Solution

Our solution is an intelligent, centralized digital marketplace for lost and found items. It enables users to report lost or found items quickly, uses AI to suggest potential matches, and facilitates secure communication.

Key Features

  • Smart Posting: Intuitive interface for reporting lost or found items with photos and descriptions.
  • AI-Powered Search: Advanced algorithms to match lost items with found listings automatically.
  • Privacy-First Design: Auto-blurring of sensitive data (like NIC/IDs) in images.
  • Secure Messaging: In-app chat to coordinate returns without revealing personal phone numbers.
  • Location Services: Map-based view to identify where items were lost or found.

4. Technology Stack

We utilize a robust and modern technology stack to ensure performance, scalability, and cross-platform availability.

Frontend

Flutter (Mobile App) HTML5/CSS3 (Web Portal) JavaScript

Backend & Database

Firebase Functions (Node.js) Google Firestore (NoSQL) Firebase Auth

AI & Services

TensorFlow / ML Kit Google Maps API Cloud Storage

5. Innovation

FindBack integrates Machine Learning via Gemini/ML Kit to analyze uploaded images. It can automatically categorize items (e.g., "Phone", "Wallet") and detect sensitive information (faces, IDs) to apply privacy filters automatically. This pro-active privacy approach combined with smart matching sets it apart from basic bulletin-board style apps.

6. Feasibility Analysis

The project leverages stable, open-source technologies and managed cloud services (Firebase), ensuring high availability and low maintenance costs. The modular architecture allows for incremental feature rollout, making the project highly feasible within the development timeline.

7. Impact Assessment

By streamlining the recovery of lost items, FindBack:

  • Reduces Economic Loss: Helps users recover expensive devices and documents.
  • Saves Time: Eliminates the need to physically visit multiple locations.
  • Promotes Community Trust: Encourages good samaritan behavior through a secure platform.
  • Enhances Privacy: Protects user identity during the search process.

8. Additional Information

Market research indicates a high demand for a centralized, digital lost-and-found solution, especially in high-density urban areas and university campuses where item loss is frequent.
